My International Map App

Welcome back to Home!

Hi, I have an idea of creating an interactable International Map, where you can get information about different countries in the World: ethnicity, language, race, population, etc.

Page 0 would be the Log-in Page / Welcome Page to returning users with their unique username & password combination, or first time user can create a new account that will be posted on the cloud. I'll have to figure out to make my website secured from hackers!

Page 1 would be the Home Page, with personalized features that you can interact with, or navigate to other pages. I was thinking of having a search bar, somewhat like Google's search bar but for different countries. If anyone has ideas for this, please throw it at me.

Page 2 is the International Map. Here, user can hover the countries for a general overview, or click on it to get a comprehensive sense of the countries. As a life-long travel enthusiast, this will be my chance of learning about different countries as I work on this. I could also include pictures, videos, a short link, or whatnot about those countries as well.

Page 3 is the US Map, with 50 states all drawn out. The concept is basically the same as the International Map, but it's more local since a lot of people here has never travelled outside of the US. I could have a button that changes the outline of the map, per se maps of tornados, winds, heat maps, NBA teams of different states. The possibilities are endless.

Page 4 is my favorite since it's a combination of the previous 2 pages. Here, you will be able to pinpoint the locations that you have traveled to as your 'unique travel collection'. I will have a button that switches between Internation and US Map, so people could personalize to their liking. I could also have tabs like "Favorites", "Country of Origin", "To-Travel List", or personal notes that you can add for future use.

Page 5 could be a mini-game related to the maps. Some additional brainstorming will be needed for this part, and based on the time frame, I might not get to this.
